Books/Series I've read recently

[does listening to an audiobook count as reading? I say yes]

The Kingkiller Chronicles (The Name of the Wind & The Wise Man's Fear) - Patrick Rothfuss

  • Not as much action as expected for a fantasy novel, but the world and magic system are some of the most interesting and coherent I've seen. Apparently book 3 will come out August 2020? We shall see.

The Rook Files (The Rook & Stiletto) - Daniel O'Malley

  • The writing itself is really witty/funny all the way through, but not at the expense of high stakes. The premise of a magical British Secret Service is really fun. I await book 3!

Sphere - Michael Crichton

  • A pretty classic sci-fi book that asks some big questions about humanity and morality.
