Games I've played recently

Persona 5 The Royal

In an effort to continuing learning Japanese, I picked up a physical copy of the game while I was at CoRL 2019! It's a lot of fun but definitely need to sit down for at least an hour each time you play.

  • Favorite Songs:

    • Groovy Incredible until the strings come in
    • Take Over
    • Swear to My Bones So much emotion in this one
