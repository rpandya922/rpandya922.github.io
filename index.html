Ravi Pandya

PhD Student

The Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University

As of fall 2020, I am a PhD student in the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University. I am grateful to be funded by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.

Previously, I was a data scientist at the Global AI Accelerator (GAIA) within Ericsson. I worked on applying machine learning to problems within telecommunications, such as using causal inference and deep reinforcement learning to optimize the performance of radio networks.

During my undergrad, I had the privilege of doing research in multiple robotics labs at Berkeley: Human-Assistive Robotic Technologies Lab under Prof. Ruzena Bajcsy, Biomimetic Millisystems Lab under Prof. Ron Fearing, and the InterACT Lab under Prof. Anca Dragan.
